

# FreshBasket — Mobile Grocery Delivery App

A premium, mobile-first vegetable & fruit delivery web app inspired by Blinkit, with a clean **blue & white** theme and stunning UI/UX. English only. Frontend with dummy data.

---

## 🏠 1. Home Screen
- **Top bar** with location pin, delivery address, and search icon
- **Search bar** with placeholder "Search for vegetables, fruits..."
- **Hero banner carousel** with promotional offers (e.g., "Fresh Mangoes 30% OFF", "Free delivery on ₹199+")
- **Category chips** — horizontal scrollable row (Leafy Greens, Root Vegetables, Exotic Fruits, Seasonal, Herbs, etc.)
- **"Fastest Delivery" badge** — "Delivery in 10 minutes" banner
- **Product sections:**
  - 🔥 Trending Now (horizontal scroll cards)
  - 🥬 Fresh Vegetables (grid)
  - 🍎 Fresh Fruits (grid)
  - 💰 Best Deals (horizontal scroll)
- **Bottom navigation bar** — Home, Categories, Cart (with badge count), Orders, Profile

## 🔍 2. Search Page
- Full-screen search with recent searches and popular suggestions
- Live filtering as user types
- Results shown as product cards

## 📂 3. Category Page
- Grid of all categories with attractive icons/images
- Tap a category to see filtered products

## 🛒 4. Product Listing Page
- Filter & sort options (Price low-high, Popularity)
- Product cards showing: image, name, weight/unit, price, discounted price, "ADD" button
- Quick quantity selector (+/−) after adding

## 📦 5. Product Detail Page
- Large product image
- Product name, price, weight options (250g, 500g, 1kg)
- "Add to Cart" button
- Product description, nutritional info
- "Similar Products" section at bottom

## 🛍️ 6. Cart Page
- List of added items with images, quantity controls, prices
- Bill summary: Item total, Delivery fee, Discount, Grand total
- Coupon code input field
- "Proceed to Checkout" button
- "You might also like" suggestions

## 📋 7. Checkout Page
- Delivery address section (dummy)
- Delivery time slot selector
- Payment method selector (COD, UPI, Card — visual only)
- Order summary
- "Place Order" button

## ✅ 8. Order Confirmation Page
- Success animation/checkmark
- Order ID, estimated delivery time
- "Track Order" button (dummy)
- "Continue Shopping" button

## 👤 9. Profile Page
- User avatar, name, phone
- My Orders, Addresses, Help & Support, About links
- Logout button

## 🎨 Design & UX Details
- **Mobile-only layout** (max-width 430px, centered on desktop)
- **Blue & white theme** with accent colors for deals/offers
- Smooth micro-animations on add-to-cart, page transitions
- Skeleton loading states for premium feel
- Rounded cards, soft shadows, modern typography
- Product images with consistent aspect ratios
- Sticky bottom nav and floating cart summary bar
- All data is hardcoded/dummy — no backend needed
